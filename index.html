I wanted to prove to myself that I could reach #1 in thr tournament. I havn't achieved it yet, but I know that I will soon.
I also participate in marching band at my school. The instrument I play is the flute and I absolutely enjoy it. Marching band is a lot, you have to make sure you're timing your steps properly with the beat and everyone else, make sure you are playing the right notes at the right time, focusing on the people around you as you play so you dont bump into someone as you're moving, and meanhile looking at your music at the same time. It requires a lot of practice, focus, and precision to be able to do it all properly.
